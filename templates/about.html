About us

  • Vision :

    • We want to reach more than 1 million students accross 10 cities.
    Finding a secondary books at minimal price is a challenge for every student. From Aplepustak you will get secondary books at best prices.

  • Mission :

    • We bring students together on this platform who willing to buy and sell their books at the same time.
    We serve to those students who wants to buy & sell their books at best price.

  • Objective :

    • A student sell their books at less than 25% of the MRP, at the same time another student purchase same books at more than 70% of the MRP.
    Due to this both students face financial loss. Considering the problems of the students we bring this platform for students to reduce such losses.
